[Azure Blockchain Service](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/blockchain/service/overview) is a managed blockchain service for building consortium networks.

To collect Azure Blockchain Service logs into a Log Analytics workspace, configure [Azure Diagnostic Settings](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/blockchain/service/monitor-azure-blockchain-service) for the resource, selecting the log categories to send to the workspace.
