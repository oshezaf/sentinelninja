2. Configure the Open in Tool Feature

  1. Navigate to the Environment section of the HUNTER Platform.

  2. Fill in te Root URI of your environment in the section labeled Microsoft Sentinel. Replace the with the IDs and Names of your Subscription, Resource Groups and Workspaces.

    https://portal.azure.com#@AzureTenantID/blade/Microsoft_OperationsManagementSuite_Workspace/Logs.ReactView/resourceId/%2Fsubscriptions%2FAzureSubscriptionID%2Fresourcegroups%2FResourceGroupName%2Fproviders%2Fmicrosoft.operationalinsights%2Fworkspaces%2F<WorkspaceName>/ 3. Click Save.
