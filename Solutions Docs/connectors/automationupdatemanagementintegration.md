[Azure Automation](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/automation/update-management/overview) is a cloud-based service for process automation, configuration management, and update management.

To collect update management data, enable [Update Management](https://learn.microsoft.com/azure/automation/update-management/overview) for the machine.
