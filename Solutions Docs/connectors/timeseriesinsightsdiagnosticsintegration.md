[Azure Time Series Insights](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/time-series-insights/overview-what-is-tsi) is an analytics platform for IoT time-series data.

To collect Azure Time Series Insights logs into a Log Analytics workspace, configure [Azure Diagnostic Settings](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/time-series-insights/time-series-insights-environment-mitigate-latency) for the resource, selecting the log categories to send to the workspace.
