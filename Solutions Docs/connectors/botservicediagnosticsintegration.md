[Azure Bot Service](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/bot-service/bot-service-overview) is a managed service for building enterprise-grade conversational AI bots.

To collect Azure Bot Service logs into a Log Analytics workspace, configure [Azure Diagnostic Settings](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/bot-service/bot-service-manage-analytics) for the resource, selecting the log categories to send to the workspace.
