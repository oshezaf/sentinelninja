[Azure Video Indexer](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/azure-video-indexer/video-indexer-overview) is an AI-powered video analytics service.

To collect Azure Video Indexer logs into a Log Analytics workspace, configure [Azure Diagnostic Settings](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/azure-video-indexer/monitor-video-indexer) for the resource, selecting the log categories to send to the workspace.
