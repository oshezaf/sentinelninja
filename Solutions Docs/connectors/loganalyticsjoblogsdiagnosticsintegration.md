[Log Analytics search jobs](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/azure-monitor/logs/manage-access) run asynchronous queries that bring results into a new search table for further analysis, enabling investigation of large datasets in archived or basic log tiers.

To collect job execution logs into a Log Analytics workspace, configure [Azure Diagnostic Settings](https://learn.microsoft.com/azure/azure-monitor/essentials/create-diagnostic-settings) on the Log Analytics workspace resource, enabling the job logs category.
