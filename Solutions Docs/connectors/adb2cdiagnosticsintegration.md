[Azure AD B2C](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/active-directory-b2c/overview) is a customer identity access management (CIAM) solution.

To collect Azure AD B2C logs into a Log Analytics workspace, configure [Azure Diagnostic Settings](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/active-directory-b2c/azure-monitor) for the resource, selecting the log categories to send to the workspace.
