Service Accounts Performing Remote PowerShell. Author: miflower. The purpose behind this detection is for finding service accounts that are performing remote powershell sessions. There are two phases to the detection: Identify service accounts, Find remote PS cmdlets being ran by these accounts. To accomplish this, we utilize DeviceLogonEvents and DeviceEvents to find cmdlets ran that meet the criteria.
