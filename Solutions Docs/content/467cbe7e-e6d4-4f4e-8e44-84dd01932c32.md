# Created CRUD S3 policy and then privilege escalation

Detected creation of new CRUD S3 policy and afterwards used one of the attach policy operations (AttachUserPolicy/AttachRolePolicy/AttachGroupPolicy). This might indicate a privilege escalation technique that attackers could use.
