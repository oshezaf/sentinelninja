Did you know you can use Emojis in Windows?. Read more here: https://davidzych.com/abusing-emoji-in-windows. Check-out who in your organization has renamed his or her computer to a Pizza or to a smiling poop. You might be amused by the results, or perhaps angry if one of your systems or scripts was broken by this... Note: this query will also return some machines with non-English charcters that are not Emojis. Credit for this query goes to miflower - thanks for bringing joy to our lives! :).
