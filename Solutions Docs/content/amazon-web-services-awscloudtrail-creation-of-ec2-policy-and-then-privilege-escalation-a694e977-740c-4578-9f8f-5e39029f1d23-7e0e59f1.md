# AWSCloudTrail - Creation of EC2 policy and then privilege escalation

Identifies creation of IAM policies that grant broad EC2 permissions or EC2 actions combined with IAM pass-role capability, followed by policy attachment activity. This pattern can indicate attempts to gain elevated cloud execution privileges and should be validated as authorized change activity.
