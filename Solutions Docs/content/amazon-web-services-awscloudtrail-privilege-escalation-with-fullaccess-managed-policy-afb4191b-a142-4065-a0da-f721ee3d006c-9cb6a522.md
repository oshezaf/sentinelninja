# AWSCloudTrail - Privilege escalation with FullAccess managed policy

Detects successful attachment of AWS managed policies containing FullAccess permissions to users, roles, or groups, excluding admin-named policies. This action can rapidly expand privilege scope and should be reviewed as potential cloud privilege escalation.
